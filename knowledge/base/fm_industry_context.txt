FM INDUSTRY CONTEXT — UK Facilities Management Sector

Sector Overview:
- UK FM market size: approximately £120bn annually
- Split: Hard FM (mechanical, electrical, HVAC, fabric) vs Soft FM (cleaning, security, catering)
- Key procurement routes: competitive tender, framework agreements, direct award, joint ventures
- Typical contract lengths: 3-5 years for managed contracts, 1-2 years for project-based
- Increasingly driven by: ESG requirements, energy efficiency mandates, net zero commitments

Typical Deal Structures:
- Total Facilities Management (TFM): single supplier for all FM services
- Bundled FM: 2-3 service lines from one supplier
- Single service: one specific service (e.g. cleaning only)
- Project-based: one-off capital works or fit-out

Key Buyer Types:
- Central government and NHS (large framework agreements, long procurement cycles)
- Local authorities (competitive tender, price-sensitive)
- Commercial real estate (relationship-driven, high value)
- Education (framework-heavy, budget-constrained)
- Defence (specialist requirements, high barriers to entry)

Sales Process Characteristics:
- Long procurement cycles for contracts >£500k (often 12-18 months from tender to award)
- Relationship-driven — incumbent has significant advantage at renewal
- Technical submissions and pricing are evaluated separately in most tenders
- Social value and ESG scoring increasingly weighted in public sector tenders
- TUPE (Transfer of Undertaking) considerations affect transition costs and timelines

Win Rate Benchmarks (UK FM sector):
- Competitive tender overall: 20-35%
- Incumbent renewal: 60-75%
- Framework call-off: 35-55%
- Direct award / sole source: 70-90%

Sales Cycle Benchmarks (working days from opportunity creation to close):
- <£100k: 30-60 days
- £100k-£500k: 90-180 days
- £500k-£2M: 180-365 days
- >£2M: 365-540 days

Revenue Concentration (typical FM provider):
- Top 10% of accounts = 60-70% of revenue
- Top 20% of accounts = 75-85% of revenue
- Bottom 50% of accounts = 5-10% of revenue

Pipeline Health Benchmarks:
- Healthy pipeline coverage ratio: 3x revenue target
- Stale opportunity threshold: 90 days without stage progression
- Average bid cost as % of contract value: 1-3%
